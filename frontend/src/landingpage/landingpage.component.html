You have a static website with a contact form but want to stay anonymous?

Formail will take you to a workflow that sends form data from your website to you without exposing personal contact information. In a few easy steps, form data will be delivered to your email inbox worry-free. No additional code required.

How it works

  1. 01Create an account and a new form to email workflow.
  2. 02Write a template email that will be filled with the form data when the form is submitted.
  3. 03Add the submit link to the form on your website.

Create an account

Enter your email address below to sign up. This email will be linked to your newly created account. You will receive a link to your dashboard where you can create new form to email workflows. The link will expire when not used frequently, but you can always request a new link with your email that will lead to your account.
